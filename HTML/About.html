About Us

We are a group of 10 curious, driven students between the ages of 12-17 with a diverse set of experiences, but a shared interest in making an impact through the fascinating world of technology and robotics.

The coach of the Pr0Teens is Jean-Francais Michaud. He has a background in mechanics and has taught various technology and robotics courses in the past! In his free time he enjoys helping kids, speaking french and car racing.

The team captain of the Pr0Teens is Jennifer. She is a grade 12 student from Mary Ward Catholic Secondary school. With her previous experience with FIRST robotics and programming, she organizes the team using her knowledge in mechanics and programming. Her experiences include Arduino, Python, and Java, and Block Programming.

ZiJi is a grade 10 student at Unionville Secondary school. ZiJi contributes to the team by designing the main parts of the robot. He has 4 years of experience with Arduino, EV3, Scratch, Java and he has many workshop skills such as soldering and woodworking.

Leo is a grade 10 student from St. Robert CHS. Contributed to mechanical and technical components of the robot, as well as configuring the manual drive mode. His past includes 4 years of programming with both JavaScript and Python, and worked with Arduino.

Jason is currently a grade 9 student at the University of Toronto Schools. He works on both the programming and mechanical parts of the robot. In the past he has worked with Lego Robotics and Arduino, he is also a self taught Python programmer.

Helen is currently a grade 11 student who attends Bayview Secondary School. She is a member of Pr0teens 18844, who primarily focuses on the programming aspect. She has past experience with EV3, Arduino and Java.

Iris is a grade 11 student at Bayview Secondary School. On Pr0teens 18844, she is responsible for programming and the hardware map. She has additional experience working with java, python, and EV3.

Sean is a grade 10 student at Bur Oak Secondary School. On Pr0teens 18844, he focuses mainly on the programming aspects of the robot, but he does some mechanic work as well. This is his second time in the robotics field, and he has some background knowledge on programming.

HB is a grade 10 student at Appleby College. On the team, he is responsible for 3D design and printing. In the past he has competed in FRC and in his free time he enjoys swimming and keyboards.

Edward Liu is a grade 11 student at the York School. He focuses on programming on Pr0teens 18844. He has experience with NXT, EV3, Java, Python, Javascript, SQL, and Visual Basic.

Aiden is a grade 10 student in St. Theresa Catholic Secondary School. He is an allrounder who does mechanics, designing, and programming on the side. He has experience in Arduino. Through this competition, he is learning more about programming in java.
