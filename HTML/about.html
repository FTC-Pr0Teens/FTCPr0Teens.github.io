About Us

We are a group of 9 curious, driven students between the ages of 13-17 with a diverse set of experiences, but a shared interest in making an impact through the fascinating world of technology and robotics.

The coach of the Pr0Teens is Jean-Francais Michaud. He has a background in mechanics and has taught various technology and robotics courses in the past! In his free time he enjoys helping kids, speaking french and car racing.

The team captain of the Pr0Teens is Leo. He is a grade 12 student from St. Robert Catholic High School. He contributed to mechanical and technical components of the robot, as well as being the robot driver during competitions. His past includes 4 years of programming with both Java and Python, and worked with Arduino.
