About Us

We are a group of 13 curious, driven students between the ages of 12-17 with a diverse set of experiences, but a shared interest in making an impact through the fascinating world of technology and robotics.

The coach of the Pr0Teens is Jean-Francais Michaud. He has a background in mechanics and has taught various technology and robotics courses in the past! In his free time he enjoys helping kids, speaking french and car racing.

The team captain of the Pr0Teens is Leo. He is a grade 11 student from St. Robert Catholic High School. He contributed to mechanical and technical components of the robot, as well as being the robot driver during competitions. His past includes 4 years of programming with both Java and Python, and worked with Arduino.

The head of mechanics is ZiJi. He is a grade 11 student at Unionville Secondary school. ZiJi contributes to the team by managing mechanics and designing the main parts of the robot. He has 5 years of experience with robotics, and experience with Arduino, EV3, Scratch, Java, C++, Python and he has many workshop skills such as soldering and woodworking. He also has experience with hackathons and networking.

HB is a grade 11 student at Appleby College. On the team, he is responsible for 3D design and printing. In the past he has competed in FRC and in his free time he enjoys swimming and keyboards.

Jason is currently a grade 10 student at the University of Toronto Schools. He works on both the programming and mechanical parts of the robot. In the past he has worked with Lego Robotics and Arduino, he is also a self taught Python programmer.

Helen is currently a grade 12 student who attends Bayview Secondary School. She is a member of Pr0teens 18844, who primarily focuses on the programming aspect. She has past experience with EV3, Arduino and Java.

Iris is a grade 12 student at Bayview Secondary School. On Pr0teens 18844, she is responsible for programming and the hardware map. She has additional experience working with Java, Python, and EV3.

Edward Liu is a grade 12 student at the York School. He focuses on programming on Pr0teens 18844. He has experience with NXT, EV3, Java, Python, Javascript, SQL, and Visual Basic.

Aidan is a grade 11 student in St. Theresa Catholic Secondary School. He is an allrounder who does mechanics, designing, and programming on the side. He has experience in Arduino, Java, and Fusion.
