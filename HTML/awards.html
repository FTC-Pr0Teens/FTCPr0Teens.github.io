FTC Award Descriptions

The Pr0teens are on their second year of FTC. This year's game, Freight Frenzy, requires us to transport freight such as cubes and spheres onto shipping hubs, spin a carousel wheel to deliver rubber ducks, and maneuver around field obstacles like pipes. Here are some of our accomplishments for this season:

Qualifier 1:

  • 1st Place in 2021 Waterloo Qualifiers Freight Frenzy

    • During the first qualifier, our team demonstrated our capabilities as a team to design, build, and program a robot to perform the various tasks required during Freight Frenzy. We were able to develop an efficient Autonomous and TeleOp strategy and carry it out successfully.

  • Connect Award (2021-2022)

    • We were also awarded for our various outreach efforts, aiming to spread knowledge of STEM throughout our community (see the Events page).
Qualifier 2:
  • 2nd Place in 2021 Freight Frenzy

    • During the second qualifier, our team made many several changes to our robot hardware and programming. We were able to carry out our strategy even more efficiently.


Last year's game, Ultimate Goal, required a robot that could shoot rings into goals of various heights, knock down "power goals," and move wobble goals around the field. Here are some of our accomplishments for Ultimate Goal:

Qualifier 1:

  • 1st Place Ranked Award (2020-2021)

    • During the first qualifier, our team demonstrated our capabilities as a team to design, build, and program a robot to perform the various tasks required during the Ultimate Goal.

  • Connect Award (2020-2021)

    • Through our work with our community and outreach, our team proved our ability to spread knowledge about FIRST and allow those in our community to find opportunities with FIRST. This included our outreach events (See "Events") and other outreach efforts like our various social media accounts.

Qualifier 2:
  • 2nd Place Ranked Award (2020-2021)

    • During our second qualifier, our team once again worked our best to create an efficient and functioning robot and earned 2nd place for Ultimate Goal.

  • Inspire Award (2020-2021)

    • During this qualifier, our team also demonstrated the qualities of a role-model for FIRST teams through our teamwork and efforts while making the robot. We made a good candidate for all of the other awards.

Provincial Championship:
  • 6th Place Ranked Award (2020-2021)

    • During the provincials, we did our best to make huge changes to our robot in order to maximize efficiency and fit our ever-changing game strategy. We had decent runs with our new robot!

  • Inspire Award (2020-2021)

    • Once again for the provincials, our team was a role-model for other FIRST teams through our teamwork and efforts. We made a good candidate for all of the other awards.
