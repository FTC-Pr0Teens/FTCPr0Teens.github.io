Events

Pr0teens Explore!

Since the start of the season, we've launched a new outreach campaign called: Pr0teens Explore! The series features various workshops teaching programming, mechanics and design. Our first workshop was held on November 25th, featuring Jason and HB teaching some 3D design basics! We've also launched a 3D design competition where students create their own designs. Our most recent workshop demonstrated some programming basics for robotics in java. So far we have over 20 submissions!


Summer Camp Outreach

Over the summer, the Pr0teens partnered worked as a part of the FTC summer institute, at Saint Ann Catholic Academy, which provided programming and STEM opportunities for over 40 underserved students. This year, we plan on creating and expanding our own camp and are currently in contact with schools to set it up.
