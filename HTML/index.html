What is FIRST?

Visit FIRST's website

FIRST is a non-profit organization that organizes robotic competitions such as First Robotics Competition, First Tech Challenge, FIRST LEGO League Challenge, FIRST LEGO League Explore, and FIRST LEGO League Discover. Below is a summary of FIRST's mission:
  • To inspire young people to be science and technology leaders and innovators
  • To help them build skills relating to STEM
  • To inspire innovation
  • To build on life skills such as self-confidence, communication, and leadership
The Pr0Teens is a team competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC).
